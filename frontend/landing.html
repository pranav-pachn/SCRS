Help Center

Smart Complaint Resolution System

SCRS gives communities and civic teams a shared workflow for reporting issues, routing them to the right role, and keeping progress visible from start to finish.

What is SCRS?

SCRS is a civic platform for reporting complaints, routing them to the right team, and keeping resolution progress transparent.

Who can use this?
  • Citizens can report issues like roads, garbage, and water problems.
  • Admins can manage, resolve, and communicate updates on complaints.
  • Authorities can monitor analytics, trends, and overall system performance.
How it works
  • Submit a complaint with the right details and evidence.
  • SCRS organizes and routes the complaint to the right workflow.
  • Priority and progress remain visible throughout resolution.
  • Users receive updates until the issue is resolved.
How to create account
  • Enter your name and email address.
  • Select the role that matches your responsibilities.
  • Create a password and confirm your account.
  • Use Get Started to enter the correct workspace.
