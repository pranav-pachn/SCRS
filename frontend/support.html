    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I report issues anonymously?

    Yes, most reports can be filed without identifying yourself. However, providing contact info helps us follow up if more detail is needed.

    How do I track my report's progress?

    Use your Case ID on the dashboard or open the My Complaints tab for real-time updates.

    Who determines the priority of my issue?

    Automated triage and city-approved severity rules categorize each issue by urgency and public impact.

    What if my issue is not in the list?

    Use the General Inquiry category under reporting, or contact Support directly from this page.
