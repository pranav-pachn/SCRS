Civixa Help Center

Need a hand with Civixa?

What is Civixa?

Civixa is the Civixa that guides citizens, administrators, and authority partners through reporting, triaging, and resolving civic issues with real-time insights and AI assistance.

Who can use it?

Citizen

Submit complaints, attach photos, and track updates until resolution.

Admin

Coordinate workflows, assign investigations, and collaborate across teams.

Authority

Monitor KPIs, approve escalations, and visualize impact citywide.

How it works

  • Register with your role (citizen, admin, authority) to unlock the matching workspace.
  • Log in, file a complaint, attach evidence, and assign it to the right team or authority.
  • Monitor automated updates, request clarifications, and close the case once resolved.

Common issues

  • Forgot password? Request a reset from the login form or contact support.
  • Seeing stale data? Refresh the dashboard or log out and back in to sync.
  • Need special permissions? Ask your administrator to elevate your account.
