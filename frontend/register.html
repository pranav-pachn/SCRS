SCRS Help Center

Need a hand with SCRS?

What is SCRS?

SCRS is the Smart Complaint Resolution System that guides citizens, administrators, and authority teams through reporting, triaging, and resolving civic issues.

Who can use it?

Citizen

Submit complaints, attach photos, and track updates until resolution.

Admin

Coordinate workflows, assign investigations, and resolve cases.

Authority

Monitor KPIs, approve escalations, and visualize impact citywide.

How it works

  • Register with your role to unlock the matching workspace.
  • Log in, file a complaint, attach evidence, and assign it to the right team.
  • Monitor updates, request clarifications, and close the case once resolved.

Common issues

  • Forgot password? Request a reset from the login flow or contact support.
  • Seeing stale data? Refresh the dashboard or log out and back in.
  • Need special permissions? Ask your administrator to elevate your account.
