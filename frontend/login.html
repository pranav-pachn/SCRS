What is SCRS?

SCRS is the Smart Complaint Resolution System that helps citizens, administrators, and authority teams report, route, and resolve civic issues with clear progress visibility.

Who can use it?

Citizen

Submit complaints, attach photos, and track updates until resolution.

Admin

Coordinate workflows, assign work, and resolve incoming complaints.

Authority

Monitor analytics, review escalations, and supervise system performance.

How it works

  • Register with your role to unlock the matching workspace.
  • Log in, file or manage complaints, and attach the evidence needed for action.
  • Track updates until the complaint is resolved and recorded.

Common issues

  • Forgot password? Use support guidance or contact the team directly.
  • Seeing stale data? Refresh the page or sign out and back in.
  • Need elevated permissions? Ask an administrator to review your role access.
