info About This Project

A smarter way to manage civic complaints

Civixa is an academic smart city project designed to modernize how citizens report and track civic issues. Built with AI-assisted classification and a multi-role architecture, it bridges the gap between citizens and municipal departments — making resolution faster, routing more accurate, and government more accountable.

Need help with Civixa?

What is Civixa?

Civixa is an AI-powered civic complaint management platform for citizens, administrators, and authority partners to report, triage, and resolve civic issues.

What issues can I report?

  • Road damage and potholes
  • Garbage and sanitation issues
  • Water supply disruptions
  • Electricity and streetlight problems
  • Public infrastructure concerns

How to get started

  1. Register and choose your role
  2. Log in to access your dashboard
  3. Submit a complaint or track existing ones
